About Debug.10.0

Debug.10.0 is Africa's biggest online coding and tech competition. Over the years, we have breed and empowered over 6000+ techpreneurs across subsaharan Africa. It consists of three-hour rounds of a variety of algorithmic challenges designed by Google engineers. Participants can compete in one or all online rounds held throughout the year, and will have the opportunity to develop and grow their programming abilities while getting a glimpse of technical skills needed for an enhanced career.
