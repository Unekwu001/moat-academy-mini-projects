About me

In 2018, Unekwu started his software engineering journey when he enrolled to the African leadership Group Software engineering program. He proceeded to securing a spot at the 2019 Udacity full stack scholarship for young africans where he ganered basic experience on SDLC (software deve lopment life cycle). By 2020, he had developed keen interest in the linux operating system. He enrolled for the ALX one-year intensive bootcamp for system engineers, where he mastered the basics of networking, devops and cloud computing.

In a bid to become a professional full-stack developer, he enrolled to Moat Academy, Ikeja, Lagos,(one of Nigeria's most sort after programming schools). Fast-forward to the present, Unekwu has solidified his foot in the industry with over 10 full-stack web projects to his name.
